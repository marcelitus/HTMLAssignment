So you want to build a company? You came to the right place

"Your time is limited, so don't waste it living someone else's life. Don't be trapped by dogma - which is living with the results of other people's thinking. Don't let the noise of other opinions drown out your own inner voice. And most important, have the courage to follow your heart and intuition. They somehow already know what you truly want to become. Everything else is secondary." -Steve Jobs

To build your own startup or to work for one. That is the question!

Posted on June 5th 2013 by Marcelo Lanzarotti - Comments

This is a hard question, it all depends!. But in general terms, if you are just getting started the ideal would be to work for a few years at a Startup to get expierence and learn form peers. Once you have built some experience now it is time to jump into building your own company! Here is a video from my favorite author Steve Blank speaking about the subject.
